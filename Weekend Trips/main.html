Weekend Trips In Europe:

One of my favorite parts about studying abroad in Europe was how easy it was to travel to different citites. Europe is really accomodating to students, so there are alot of discounts made available for either train tickets or airfare. For cheaper airfare, my friends and I would fly by RyanAir or EasyJet. I preferred EasyJet personally, because they are more leanent with the weight of your luggage. Although a little more expensive, trains are another great alternative. I went to Berlin, Prague, Brussels, and Zurich all by train and the sights were amazing on the way there. Therefore, I would definitely recommend travelling by train if you are ever in Europe.
